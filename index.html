About Me

Hello! I'm Joven McLord Valdez, but you can call me Mac. I completed my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Iloilo Science and Technology University. Unlike many computer science majors who primarily focus on coding and programming, my personal emphasis was on graphics design, along with a bit of HTML and CSS. Designing has always been my passion, and I have been continuously exploring different styles. I am constantly open to learning more and strive to expand my knowledge every day. I am eager to acquire skills beyond my primary interests and broaden my horizons.
